Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how OakleyGasAndSave collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Effective Date: April 26, 2026 · Last Updated: April 26, 2026

OakleyGasAndSave ("we," "us," or "our") operates a gas station and convenience store located at 123 Main Street, Oakley, CA 94561, and the website oakleygasandsave.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our Site, shop at our store, fuel up at our station, or participate in our rewards program.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

a. Information You Provide

  • Identifiers — Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address (e.g., when you sign up for our Rewards program or contact us).
  • Payment Information — Credit or debit card number and billing details when you make a purchase or pay at the pump. We do not store full card numbers; payment processing is handled by PCI-compliant third-party processors.
  • Rewards & Loyalty Data — Transaction history, fuel grade preferences, reward points, and redemption records linked to your membership.
  • Communications — Any messages, feedback, or inquiries you send us via email, phone, or the contact form on our Site.

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device & Browser Data — IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type, and screen resolution.
  • Usage Data — Pages viewed, links clicked, time spent on pages, referring URL, and other browsing behavior on the Site.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies — We use cookies, pixels, and local storage to remember preferences, analyze traffic, and improve the Site. See Section 6 below.
  • Geolocation — Approximate location derived from your IP address to show relevant local information.

c. Information from On-Site Systems

  • Security Camera Footage — CCTV cameras operate 24/7 at our fuel islands, parking lot, store interior, and ATM area for safety and loss prevention.
  • Point-of-Sale Data — Transaction amounts, items purchased, fuel volume, and payment method associated with in-store and pump purchases.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information for the following purposes:

  • Process fuel and in-store purchases and issue receipts.
  • Administer our Rewards program — tracking points, applying discounts, and sending membership communications.
  • Respond to your questions, feedback, and support requests.
  • Improve our Site, services, product selection, and customer experience.
  • Send promotional offers, fuel price updates, and newsletters (only with your consent; you may opt out at any time).
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, theft, and security incidents.
  • Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal obligations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers — Payment processors, email marketing platforms, website analytics providers, and IT support vendors who process data on our behalf under contractual confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements — When required by law, subpoena, court order, or to cooperate with law enforcement or government agencies.
  • Safety & Fraud Prevention — To protect the rights, property, or safety of OakleyGasAndSave, our customers, or the public.
  • Business Transfers — In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our business, your information may be transferred to the successor entity.
  • With Your Consent — For any other purpose with your prior consent.

4. Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) provide you with the following rights:

  • Right to Know — You may request the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources, the purposes, and the third parties with whom we shared it.
  • Right to Delete — You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Correct — You may request that we correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing — We do not sell your personal information or share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. If this changes, we will provide a "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link on our Site.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Information — You may limit the use of sensitive personal information (such as precise geolocation or financial account details) to what is necessary for providing services.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — We will not deny services, charge different prices, or provide a lesser quality of service because you exercise your privacy rights.

How to Submit a Request

You or your authorized agent may submit a privacy request by:

  • Emailing us at privacy@oakleygasandsave.com
  • Calling us at (925) 000-0000
  • Visiting us in person at 123 Main Street, Oakley, CA 94561

We will verify your identity by matching at least two pieces of information you provide against the records we maintain. We will acknowledge your request within 10 business days and respond substantively within 45 calendar days.

Our Rewards program offers fuel discounts (3¢ per gallon) and promotional deals in exchange for your enrollment and transaction data. You may opt out of the Rewards program at any time by contacting us. The value of the discount is reasonably related to the data collected and is calculated based on average program participation and redemption rates.

5. California "Shine the Light"

Under California Civil Code Section 1798.83, California residents may request information about our disclosure of personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. We do not share personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. If this practice changes, we will update this section and provide an opt-out mechanism.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our Site uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies — Required for the Site to function (e.g., navigation, security).
  • Analytics Cookies — Help us understand how visitors use the Site (e.g., Google Analytics). These collect anonymized usage data.
  • Preference Cookies — Remember your settings and choices for a better experience.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect Site functionality. We honor "Do Not Track" browser signals where technically feasible.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Specifically:

  • Transaction records: 7 years (tax and legal compliance).
  • Rewards program data: Duration of membership plus 12 months after cancellation.
  • Security camera footage: 30–90 days on a rolling basis, unless preserved for an active investigation.
  • Website analytics data: 26 months.

After the retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption on all Site pages.
  • PCI DSS-compliant payment processing.
  • Restricted employee access on a need-to-know basis.
  • Regular security reviews of our systems and vendors.

No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Site and services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., Google Maps, social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will post the revised policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
