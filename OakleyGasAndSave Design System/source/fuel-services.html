Fuel Grades

Choose the Right Fuel for Your Vehicle

We carry all major octane grades plus diesel to keep every vehicle running at its best.

Regular Unleaded (87)

The standard fuel for most passenger cars, SUVs, and trucks. Regular 87 octane is the most economical option and works perfectly for engines that don't require higher-octane fuel. Ideal for daily commuters and everyday driving around Oakley and East Contra Costa County.

Plus Midgrade (89)

A step up from regular for vehicles that benefit from a slightly higher octane rating. Plus 89 is recommended for some newer sedans and light trucks. Check your owner's manual to see if midgrade is the right choice for your engine.

Premium Unleaded (91)

High-octane fuel designed for performance and luxury vehicles with high-compression or turbocharged engines. Premium 91 helps prevent engine knocking and ensures optimal performance. Required by many European imports and sports cars.

Diesel

Ultra-low sulfur diesel (ULSD) for diesel-powered trucks, SUVs, and commercial vehicles. Our diesel pumps are designed with wide lanes for easy access by larger vehicles. Perfect for fleet operators and truckers traveling along Highway 4.
